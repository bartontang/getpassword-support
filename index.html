About GetPassword

GetPassword is a secure and user-friendly password generator and manager. It helps you create strong passwords and safely store them on your device.

Key Features:

  • Generate secure passwords with customizable length and characters
  • Save and manage passwords locally
  • Quick copy and paste functionality
  • Password visibility toggle
  • Multi-language support

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is GetPassword safe to use?

A: Yes. GetPassword stores all passwords locally on your device and doesn't transmit any data over the internet. We use secure encryption methods to protect your data.

Q: What happens to my passwords if I delete the app?

A: All stored passwords will be permanently deleted when you uninstall the app. Please make sure to backup important passwords before deleting the app.

Q: How many passwords can I store?

A: Free version allows storing up to 3 passwords. VIP users can store unlimited passwords.

Q: How do I restore my VIP purchase?

A: Simply tap the "Restore Purchase" button in the app to restore your VIP status.
